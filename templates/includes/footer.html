About Us

Established in the 2010, Stumps & Studs has long been established as one of the leading UK specialist retailer of sports equipment, fashionable branded footwear and clothing.

What started as a single shop in Ipswich, Suffolk has now expanded across the UK, and by 2019, Stumps & Studs had opened a total of 21 stores nationwide. Stumps & Studs is regarded as one of the most innovative merchandiser of sportswear with the best and most exclusive stylish range.

Our online store attracts customers from across Europe and beyond and we look forward to opening first outlet outside of the UK very soon . . .
